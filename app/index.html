3. DFINITY Donation Procedure

3.1 Qualification

  1. Any contribution made to DFINITY during the Donation Rounds as described below is qualified as a non-refundable Donation.

3.2 Donation Rounds and Donation Periods

  1. Donations will be collected in two rounds, a “Seed Round” and a “Main Round” (together “Donation Rounds”).
  2. The Seed round will start on 19th of December 2016.
  3. The Main Round is scheduled to take place on the 1st of March 2017, but can be delayed maximum three times by each 1 month in the discretion of DFINITY using a privileged call it can make into the FDC (see section 3.5).
  4. The two Donation Rounds last a maximum of 6 weeks (“Donation Period”). For both Donation Rounds, Donation targets (“Donation Target”) have been defined (see section 3.4).
  5. No minimal or maximal Donation amounts per Donor exist.

3.3 Accepted Currencies

  1. Donations can be made using ether (ETH), bitcoin (BTC) or CHF.
  2. Donations in ETH or BTC must be made by using the Chrome DFINITY Stiftung Donation Extension Software (“Donation Software”) provided by DFINITY. Any ETH or BTC Donation to DFINITY without using the Donation Software will not lead to a recommendation for DFN allocation as set forth below (see section 4).
  3. If the Donor wishes to make a Donation in CHF, it must be more than CHF 100’000 in amount and must be pre-approved and verified by DFINITY. Any CHF Donation to DFINITY without following this procedure will not lead to a recommendation for DFN allocation as set forth below (see section 4).

3.4 Target Amounts

  1. The Donation Target for the Seed Round is CHF 1 Mio (CHF 1’000’000).
  2. The Donation Target for the Main Round is CHF 20 Mio (CHF 20’000’000).
  3. If a Donation Round reaches its Donation Target before the end date of the Donation Period, the FDC will automatically stop accepting additional Donations approx. [a]1 hour after the Donation Target has been reached.

3.5 Foundations Donations Contract (FDC)

  1. Donations made to DFINITY during the Donation Rounds are being recorded in an Ethereum smart contract called the FDC[b].
  2. The FDC will record the equivalent "value" of Donations in CHF (Swiss francs). The CHF equivalent for ETH or BTC shall be calculated using the time-averaged price provided by CoinMarketCap or equivalent service chosen by DFINITY at the time of the Donation[c].
  3. When Donations are recorded by the FDC, a multiplier is applied for the purpose of DFN allocation (“Donation Multiplier”). The application of the Donation Multiplier results in the amount of “FDC Allocation Units” for each Donation. A fixed Donation Multiplier of 2.5X will be applied to Donations made in the Seed Round (thus a Donation of 1 CHF will be recorded with 2.5 FDC Allocation Units). For each week of the Main Round, a different Donation Multiplier will be applied:
  1. Week 1: Donation Multiplier of 1.25X
  2. Week 2: Donation Multiplier of 1.20X
  3. Week 3: Donation Multiplier of 1.15X
  4. Week 4: Donation Multiplier of 1.10X
  5. Week 5: Donation Multiplier of 1.05X
  6. Week 6: No Donation Multiplier
  1. Upon a successful Donation being made, a digital receipt will be created by the FDC that reflects the quantity of the DFN tokens DFINITY shall propose to allocate to the Donor in accordance to his respective amount of FDC Allocation Units (“Donation Receipt)”. Such allocation shall be proposed in accordance to the mechanism set forth below (see section 4). The time that it takes for the FDC to create the receipt depends upon whether the Donor used BTC, ETH or CHF, and mechanisms inside the FDC and other technology used to collect Donations.

4. DFN Allocation Proposal

  1. When DFINITY judges in its sole discretion both that The Client is sufficiently developed to launch the DFINITY Network and that it is a good time to launch the DFINITY Network, it will issue a public announcement recommending what the Genesis State should be. In the Genesis State it shall recommend the allocations of DFN as set forth below.
  2. It remains in the community’s discretion to adopt or not to adopt the Genesis State that DFINITY recommends. DFINITY cannot guarantee to Donors that they shall have any allocation and/or creation of DFN as set forth in these terms.
  3. In the Genesis State, DFINITY shall recommend the allocations of DFN according to the principles set forth below and under para 30 and 31:
  • Pool A: 80% of the “Total DFN Amount” allocated by the Genesis State shall be allocated to Donors who made Donations to DFINITY during the Donation Rounds as recorded by the FDC.

For Pool A, DFINITY will recommend that for each FDC Allocation Unit (see para 26) 10 (ten) DFN are created and allocated to the Donors in accordance to their respective amount of FDC Allocation Units recorded by the FDC during the Donation Rounds.

  • Pool B: 20% of the Total DFN Amount shall be reserved for an allocation at the complete discretion of DFINITY. E.g. for its own purposes or in return for Donations from special persons, organizations and partners who made early financial, development or management contributions or provided other support resources or guarantees that were needed to realize the current design of the DFINITY Network (“Early Contributions”).

For Pool B, DFINITY will recommend that - since the total amount of DFN created for Pool A represents 80% of the Total DFN Amount - an additional 25% of the total amount of DFN of Pool A will be created and allocated to DFINITY and/or other parties chosen by DFINITY. (For example, if Pool A consists of a total of 8’000 DFN, 2’000 DFN for Pool B are created in the Genesis State, resulting in a Total DFN Amount of 10’000 DFN).
